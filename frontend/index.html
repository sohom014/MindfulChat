About MindfulChat

Our Mission

At MindfulChat, we believe everyone deserves a compassionate listener, available 24/7. Mental health is just as important as physical health, yet access to quick, non-judgmental support is often limited.

Our AI is built on deeply trained natural language models designed strictly to be supportive. It uses advanced sentiment analysis to detect when you might be overwhelmed, anxious, or experiencing deeper distress, adapting its tone to give grounding and calming advice.

Important Disclaimer

MindfulChat is an AI tool meant for supplementary support. It is not a replacement for professional medical advice, therapy, or psychiatric help. In emergencies, please seek immediate human professional help.

Crisis Resources & Help

If you or someone you know is struggling, there is help available right now.

AASRA

Call 9820466726. A 24x7 helpline for individuals facing emotional distress or suicidal thoughts.

NIMHANS Toll-Free

Call 080-46110007. National Institute of Mental Health and Neurosciences national helpline.

Vandrevala Foundation

Call 9999 666 555. 24x7 free mental health helpline available in multiple Indian languages.

Kiran Helpline

Call 1800-599-0019. Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment's 24x7 mental health rehabilitation helpline.
